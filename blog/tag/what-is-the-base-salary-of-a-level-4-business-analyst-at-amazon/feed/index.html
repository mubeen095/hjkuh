Level 4 is a key point in Amazon’s corporate ladder structure, representing roles typically filled by recent graduates. Specifically, a Level 4 Amazon Business Analyst’s salary is usually in the range of 9 Lakhs per annum, with potential variations that can extend between 15 Lakhs and 22 Lakhs per annum. Please note that these figures can fluctuate based on location, experience, and other factors
