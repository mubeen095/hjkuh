In the world of web development, choosing between MERN (MongoDB, Express.js, React, Node.js) and MEAN (MongoDB, Express.js, Angular, Node.js) stacks is a big decision. One important factor to consider is the salaries associated with each stack. Let’s explore and compare the average earnings and pay ranges for developers working with MERN and MEAN stacks. Understanding the salary landscape can help developers and businesses make informed decisions about which stack aligns better with their goals.

Salary Of Mern Stack And Mean Stack

In India, the national average salary for Full-Stack Developers is ₹582,659 per year. Freshers start at ₹375,000, while those with 1-4 years of experience earn around ₹553,006. Mid-level professionals (5-10 years) can expect ₹1,000,000 to ₹1,500,000 per year.

For MERN Developers, the average salary is ₹800,000 annually. Entry-level positions start at ₹550,000, while experienced developers can earn up to ₹1,600,000 per year.

MEAN Stack Developers in India earn between ₹530,000 to ₹640,000 per year. Freshers (0-2 years) start at ₹370,000 to ₹500,000, those with 4-6 years of experience earn ₹680,000 to ₹920,000, and specialists with 8-10 years can make ₹1,500,000 to ₹2,200,000 annually.

FAQ- Mern Stack Vs Mean Stack Salary

Q1. Is MEAN stack better than MERN stack?

Ans. Maintaining a complete architecture is crucial in web development, and that’s why the MEAN stack is often preferred. It provides an organized framework that supports the MVC (Model-View-Controller) architecture. While the MERN stack also supports MVC design, the MEAN stack is considered more convenient for managing code and upgrades.

Q2. Which is easy MERN or MEAN?

Ans. Learning the MEAN stack can be challenging due to its steep learning curve, while the MERN stack tends to have a more accessible learning curve. Additionally, in terms of data binding, the MEAN stack supports both one-way and two-way data binding, providing more flexibility. On the other hand, the MERN stack only supports one-way data binding.

Q3. Can I get a job with MERN stack?

Ans. In web development, you deal with front-end, back-end, and databases. Among web technologies, MERN Stack is a top choice. If you’re a MERN Stack Developer, you’re set for a promising career as it’s a powerful and highly sought-after skill in the industry.
