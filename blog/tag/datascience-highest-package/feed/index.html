In the world of data science, there are several companies known for offering great pay to attract top talent. These companies value the important role data scientists play in discovering insights and driving innovation. Whether they are high-tech startups or big industry players, these ten companies are recognized for providing competitive packages that make working in data science not only fulfilling but also rewarding. Let’s take a closer look at these companies and the opportunities they offer for those looking to make a significant impact in the field of data science.

1.Amazon

Global e-commerce giant Amazon needs data science professionals to do tasks ranging from supply chain optimization, inventory, and sales forecasting to the detection of fraud. The tech giant suggests that data scientists at Amazon form the link between business and technical sides. 

Salary: Rs 5 lakh to Rs 45.57 lakh | Rs 15.56 lakh (average) 

2.Flipkart

Indian e-commerce giant Flipkart will provide jobs to data scientists across teams. Their roles that have to handle are from building Systemic Intelligence across Flipkart products to uncovering and redefining shopping trends in the country. The data science team is responsible for designing capabilities and solutions across the supply chain, fintech, consumer experience, search and discovery, demand shaping, and fraud modeling.

3. Walmart 

Flipkart’s parent company Walmart has over 29 wholesale stores spread across the country in the name of ‘Best Price’. Data science professionals at Walmart will have to discover new and innovative ways of applying data across businesses while using automation to improve processes. Data scientists at Walmart are responsible for mixing technology and retail. 

Salary: Rs 14.5 lakh to Rs 33.5 lakh | Rs 24.6 lakh (average) 

4. Hewlett Packard Enterprise

The goal of Hewlett-Packard Enterprise, or HPE, is to help customers manage and assess their data. It will enable them with advanced tech solutions, services, and consumption models to work on the available data. 

Salary: Rs 10 lakh to Rs 31 lakh | Rs 21,28,671 (average) 

5. IBM

6. Deloitte

Deloitte will give end-to-end business solutions for data science practitioners. It will ensure that analytics is in its DNA, and thus, Deloitte’s expertise lies in providing smart insights for stronger outcomes.

7. Accenture

Accenture has a diverse team of data scientists and AI experts to leverage the power of AI and bring in changes in companies and in society at large. Its team of data scientists apply ethical and responsible AI to measure the impact of businesses. 

Salary: Rs 1.87 lakh to Rs 31 lakh | Rs 10.20 lakh (average)

8. [24]7.ai

The Customer Experience software company [24]7.ai will use AI and ML to understand and analyze customer intent. It helps companies to make personalized, predictive, and seamless customer experiences across channels. Data science professionals at [24]7.ai are responsible for building and defining AI models into products and offerings.

The fact that Netflix tops the list of tech companies offering high salaries may not be surprising. Netflix has built a strong reputation for compensating its tech professionals generously, with the understanding that these employees are expected to deliver outstanding performance.
