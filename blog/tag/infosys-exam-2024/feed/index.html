What is the Infosys Interview Process?

To get a job at Infosys, you need to go through these steps:

  1. Online Test: First, you’ll have to take an online exam. This test will check your skills – logical thinking, English, math, and coding (depending on the job role). The questions can be quite difficult.
  2. Technical Interview: If you clear the online test, you’ll be called for a technical interview. Here, experts from Infosys will assess your technical knowledge and problem-solving abilities related to the job role.
  3. HR Interview: The final round is an interview with Infosys’s human resources team. They will evaluate your overall personality, communication skills, and fitment for the company’s culture.

Performing well in all three rounds is crucial to get hired by Infosys. The online test and interviews are designed – to assess your skills and potential for the job.
