Which Statement Best Describes The Goal Of DevOps?

Ans. Option B) One goal of DevOps is to establish an environment where releasing more reliable applications faster and more frequently can occur is the Correct Answer

This statement accurately represents one of the central objectives of DevOps, which is to enhance the ability to release software applications more quickly while maintaining or improving their reliability and quality. DevOps focuses on streamlining the development and deployment process, automating tasks, and fostering collaboration between development and operations teams to achieve this goal.
