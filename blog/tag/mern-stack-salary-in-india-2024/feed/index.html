MERN Stack Salary In India

The average salary for MERN Stack developers in India is ₹1,200,000 per year, equivalent to ₹481 per hour. Entry-level positions typically start at ₹698,000 per year, while highly experienced professionals can earn up to ₹2,200,000 per year.

This salary range reflects the compensation levels for individuals working with the MERN (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js) stack, showcasing the demand and remuneration for skills in this technology stack in the Indian job market. It’s important to note that salaries can vary based on factors such as location, company size, and individual skills and expertise.

MERN Stack Lowest Salary in India 2024

MERN Stack Developers in India, with less than 1 year to 5 years of experience, have a salary range from ₹1.0 Lakhs to ₹8.5 Lakhs. This data is based the various reports. The average annual salary stands at ₹3.9 Lakhs.

MERN Stack Medium Salary in India 2024

A mid-career MERN Stack Developer with 4-9 years of experience is ₹9.3 Lakhs per year. This figure reflects the compensation level for professionals who have gained significant experience and expertise in working with the MERN (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js) stack. Mid-career professionals often bring a depth of knowledge and skills, contributing to their earning potential.

MERN Stack Highest Salary in India 2024

An experienced MERN Stack Developer with 10-20 years of experience is ₹14.1 Lakhs per year. This figure depends on the professionals who have extensive experience and expertise in working with the MERN (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js) stack.

Experienced developers with a decade or more in the field often bring a wealth of knowledge, advanced skills, and a track record of successful project contributions, contributing to their higher earning potential.

FAQ- MERN Stack Salary in India 2024

Q1. What is the salary of MERN stack developer in TCS?

Ans. TCS MERN Stack Developer salaries in India range from ₹3.5 Lakhs to ₹4.4 Lakhs, with an average annual salary of ₹4.0 Lakhs. These estimates are based on 7 latest salaries from various TCS employees.

Q2.Which is better MERN stack or full stack?

Ans. Full-stack developers excel in all aspects of software development across various domains. In contrast, MERN Stack is specific to web development, and MERN stack developers specialize in JavaScript and back-end frameworks for web applications. Full Stack development is generally considered more versatile and comprehensive in terms of skill set and application across different types of projects.

Q3. What is the salary of Amazon MERN stack developer?

Ans. An Amazon MERN Full Stack Developer in India is between ₹0.3 Lakhs to ₹45.0 Lakhs, with an average annual salary of ₹11.7 Lakhs
