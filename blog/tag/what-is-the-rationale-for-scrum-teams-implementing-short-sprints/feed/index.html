During a sprint, a team works on a specific goal, usually making a better version of a product or software. Short sprints help teams coordinate and get feedback often, making it easier to fix any issues that come up. It’s a way to break work into manageable pieces and make continuous improvements. Remember, Scrum is a part of the bigger idea of agile development.
