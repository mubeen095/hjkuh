Artificial Intelligence is associated with which generation?

Ans. Option B)Fifth Generation is the Correct Answer.

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is most closely associated with the Fifth Generation of computing. The Fifth Generation project was an initiative in Japan in the 1980s to develop advanced computer systems that could use knowledge-based AI techniques to solve complex problems. While the specific goals of the Fifth Generation project were not fully realized, it played a significant role in advancing AI research and development, particularly in areas like expert systems and natural language processing.
