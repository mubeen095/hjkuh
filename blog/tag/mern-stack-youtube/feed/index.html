Q1. Is MERN stack still in demand?

Ans. The MERN stack remains highly sought after in web development due to its versatility, open-source nature, and scalability. For those aiming to become proficient developers, ShapeMySkills stands out as the premier MERN Stack training institute in Noida.

Q2. Is MERN stack good for career?

Ans. Absolutely, the MERN stack is indeed a strong choice for developing dynamic web applications, particularly single-page applications (SPAs). In a full stack development approach, a single developer is responsible for handling all aspects of a web application, covering the front-end (React), back-end (Node.js with Express), and database (MongoDB). This approach offers several advantages, including streamlined communication between different layers of the application, better understanding of the entire development process, and increased efficiency in addressing issues across the entire stack. MERN’s open-source nature and the shared use of JavaScript throughout the stack also contribute to the ease of development and maintenance.

Q3. Is MERN stack easy to learn?

Ans. Learning the MERN stack can be challenging, particularly for beginners, as it involves mastering MongoDB, Express.js, React, and Node.js. However, the initial hurdle often lies in acquiring foundational skills like JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. Before delving into MERN, building proficiency in these basics is crucial.
