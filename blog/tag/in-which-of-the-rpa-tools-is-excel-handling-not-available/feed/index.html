The RPA tools in which Excel handling is not available are Excel Automation and Macros.

The full form of RPA is Robotic Process Automation. This is the latest technology and the field where the interesting development regarding Business Process Management took place. Whereas, in this automation process, most of the business operations are held with the help of robots in order to reduce human interference. We know that all the RPA tools are subdivided into different types of tools. They are Self Learning Tools, Cognitive automation bots, Programmable Solution bots, and Excel automation and Macros.
