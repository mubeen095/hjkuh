Oracle Application Process

Candidates can refer to the Oracle application process added here and understand the steps to apply for their job at Oracle. Understand the steps to apply for the Oracle Application process and start your application.

1. Go to Oracle Careers and search for the required job

Initially, you need to go to the Oracle careers and check the jobs that suit you and the suitable location to proceed with your application.

2. Read the Oracle job description and apply if you fit the Oracle Eligibility Criteria.

Then, you need to understand the job description and if you have the skills and experience mentioned in the Oracle Job description. You can start applying for the job.

3. ‘Click Apply now’ if you want to proceed with the Oracle Application form

To proceed further with the Oracle Application, you should click ”Apply now”, which will be shown below the Oracle Job description. Read about Oracle company, benefits, and perks before applying for the Oracle job.

4. Create an account with your email ID to access the Oracle Application

Interested candidates can create a profile using the Personal Mail-Id and tick the box near I agree with the terms and conditions. Then, click next to move further with the Oracle Application.

On the other hand, if you want to cancel, check the other job role. Candidates can do that by clicking the cancel option added. Then, you will be headed back to the Oracle careers.

5. Enter your Contact Details for the Oracle Application Process

Candidates looking to work at Oracle should fill in the Contact details such as First Name, Last Name, mail ID, and Phone number. Enter the accurate detail carefully and read it carefully before moving into the next session.

Furthermore, you can also apply for Oracle Application through LinkedIn, Indeed, or directly with the Resume. This will automatically add your details below. Thus, the Application process will be completed quickly.

6. The Next step is to add education details, experience, and skills

After filling in the contact details, go to the next section and add your educational qualifications, experience, skills, and add-on certifications. Then you can also upload the Resume and cover letter. Adding the Resume is mandatory to proceed further in the Oracle Application process. candidates can also add the URL of the certification if needed. Fill in all the information in the section correctly and read again before moving into the next session.

7. The Final step is to enter the additional information

Candidates can also add the extra information of the joining date in the next section. You also need to answer a few questions provided in the last section such as nationality, gender, and diversity. It is not compulsory to fill in the questions related to gender and diversity questions. However, among the questions asked, you must fill the section with the star mark near it, those are the mandatory questions. If you are in a hurry and approaching the last date to fill in the Oracle application form, you can fill in the required questions and proceed further to apply.

Additionally, you can tick the check box added below the application form if you need to receive any job opportunities in Oracle. You can also get another check box about ” I agree to receive the career mail with news and events”.

8. ‘Click Submit’ after filling in all the required information

Applicants must add their Full name as the E-Signature before submitting the Oracle application. You need to read the information carefully and review your education and employment history as it will be required during the background verification. You need to carefully read once again the details entered before submitting the Oracle application. If there is any error in your Mail-Id or contact no, you can correct it now, as all the hiring details will be sent to your mail ID.

After reading the statement below the E-signature, you can click submit and then, your application will be sent to the Oracle recruiting team for review.

Oracle Application Status

Candidates can view the Oracle Application status in the Oracle careers. You must log – in the Oracle careers and check the Oracle Application status. It will be shown as under consideration or selected in the Oracle Application status.

The “Under Consideration” status means that your job application is still active and being evaluated for one or more of the open positions within the same hiring pipeline. A hiring pipeline is a group of related job openings being filled together, often for similar roles or within the same department or team.

When your application is marked as “Under Consideration,” – hiring team is reviewing your qualifications, experience, and other application materials to determine if you might be a good fit for any of the open roles in that pipeline. This status does not necessarily mean that you are being considered for the specific job you initially applied for, but rather that your application is being considered for any of the related positions within that hiring pipeline.

During this stage, the hiring team may conduct initial screenings, review resumes and cover letters, check references, or schedule interviews, to further evaluate candidates. Your application remains active and under consideration until a decision is made to either move you forward in the hiring process for a specific role or to remove you from consideration for that particular pipeline of job openings.

It’s important to note that the “Under Consideration” status does not guarantee that you will receive a job offer, it does mean that your application is still being actively reviewed and evaluated alongside other candidates for the available positions within that hiring pipeline.

Summing up, When you hit the “Submit” button in the Oracle Application form, you must confirm that everything you entered is truthful and accurate. Further, if you enter incorrect details, your application is rejected or you could be fired later if hired. By applying, you permit Oracle to double-check any information you provided, through references, and background checks. If you are offered a job, you agree to follow all of Oracle’s rules and policies as an employee. Lying or misleading information could disqualify you, so be honest throughout the hiring process.
