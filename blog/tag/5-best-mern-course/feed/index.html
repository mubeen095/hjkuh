Q1. What is the best way to learn MERN?

Ans. Becoming a MERN Developer starts with mastering three foundational front-end languages: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Q2. Can I learn MERN stack in 3 months?

Ans. Learning MERN can take a few months for an experienced JavaScript programmer. However, the duration may vary based on the student’s existing knowledge of JavaScript and coding fundamentals. For those needing more training, especially in advanced skills, full-time study for several months may be required to master the MERN stack thoroughly.

Q3. Is MERN stack tough or easy?
