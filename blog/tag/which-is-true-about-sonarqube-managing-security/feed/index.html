Which Is True About Sonarqube Managing Security?

Ans. Option B) Rules are collected in Quality profile is the Correct Answer

Rules in SonarQube are indeed collected and organized in what is known as Quality Profiles. Quality Profiles are sets of rules and configurations used to analyze code. They define the coding standards, coding rules, and quality gates that are applied during code analysis. Quality Profiles can be customized and associated with projects or specific branches of your codebase to ensure that the appropriate coding rules are enforced for different parts of your application. This allows you to tailor the analysis to the specific requirements of your project.
