Which Of The Following Is An Antivirus

Answer. The correct Answer is Option D.

A) Norton: Norton is a well-known antivirus software developed by Norton LifeLock (formerly Symantec). It offers comprehensive security features, including real-time protection against viruses, malware, and other online threats. Norton is a reputable antivirus solution used by many individuals and organizations to safeguard their computers and data.

B) Quick Heal: Quick Heal is another antivirus software that provides protection against viruses, malware, and various online threats. It includes features such as antivirus scanning, firewall protection, and more. Quick Heal is a popular antivirus choice, particularly in regions like India, where it is headquartered.

C) K7: K7 is yet another antivirus software that offers antivirus and anti-malware protection. It is known for its capabilities in detecting and removing various types of threats. K7 is used by individuals and businesses to secure their digital assets.

So, all three options (A) Norton, (B) Quick Heal, and (C) K7 are examples of antivirus software solutions. Therefore, the correct answer is (D) “All of these” because all three options are valid antivirus software choices.
