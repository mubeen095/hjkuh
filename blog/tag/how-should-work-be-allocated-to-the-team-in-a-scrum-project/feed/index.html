Instead, when the client provides detailed requirements, team members with the relevant expertise and skills volunteer for the tasks they want to work on. This approach fosters self-organization and empowers team members to choose the work they are best suited for and interested in. It promotes a sense of ownership and responsibility among team members, which is fundamental to the Agile and Scrum methodologies.
