When Integrating Word And Excel, Word Is Usually The _____?

Option d) Both b and C is the Correct Answer.

When integrating Word and Excel, Microsoft Word can indeed serve as both the client (where it consumes data from Excel) and the destination (where data from Excel is placed or displayed). This duality is often the case when creating documents that incorporate Excel data.

So, the answer “Both b and c” (option D) can be considered correct in this context, as Word can take on both roles.
