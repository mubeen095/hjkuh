Oracle Interview Process in 2024

The hiring process at Oracle typically begins with a phone interview, followed by an online skills assessment and tests. If you perform well in these initial stages, you will proceed to two technical interviews where your knowledge and abilities will be thoroughly evaluated. After successfully clearing the technical rounds, you will have a final interview with someone from the human resources (HR) department.

However, this comprehensive process is designed so that Oracle selects the most qualified candidates for their open positions. On average, the entire hiring process, from the initial phone screening to the final HR interview, takes approximately one to one and a half months, ranging from 33 to 41 days. This timeline allows Oracle to carefully assess each applicant and make informed hiring decisions for their organization.

In conclusion, getting hired at Oracle is a multi-step process that requires patience and preparation. The company takes its time to thoroughly evaluate candidates through phone interviews, online assessments, technical interviews, and a final HR interview. This comprehensive approach helps Oracle identify the best talent for their organization. While the process may seem lengthy, taking an average of 33 to 41 days, it is designed to ensure that both the company and the candidate make an informed decision.

Ultimately, the Oracle hiring process aims to find the right fit, where the candidate’s skills and experience align with the company’s needs and values. By understanding and navigating this process, candidates can increase their chances of landing their desired role at Oracle.

Oracle Exam 2024 – FAQs

Q1.What is the overview of the Oracle Database?

Ans. An Oracle Database, also known as Oracle RDBMS, is a system that stores different types of data in an organized way, with connections between the types. Its main job is to keep related information safe and easy to get when needed.

Q2.What is Oracle’s main product?

Ans. Oracle is the only company that provides a full set of connected cloud applications and a cloud infrastructure platform. Oracle Cloud has everything you need to move, create, and manage your IT systems, whether working with existing business tasks or new cloud-based applications and data systems.

Q3.Which database is used in Oracle?

Ans. Oracle Database is a database type called an RDBMS (Relational Database Management System). When an RDBMS includes features like user-defined types, inheritance, and polymorphism, it is called an object-relational database management system (ORDBMS).

Q4.What is Oracle’s full form?

Ans. The ORACLE, which stands for Oak Ridge Automatic Computer and Logical Engine, was an early computer built by Oak Ridge National Laboratory. It was based on the IAS architecture created by John von Neumann.

Q5.What is Oracle mostly used for?

Ans. Data Management: Oracle Database Software offers strong tools for handling data. It helps store, change, retrieve, and protect data. It supports different kinds of data, including organized and unorganized data.

Q6.What is Oracle best known for?

Ans. The company is famous for its Oracle database software, a system for managing data, and computer systems and software like Solaris and Java, which it got when it bought Sun Microsystems in 2010. Oracle is located in Redwood Shores, California.

Q7.What is the latest version of Oracle?

Ans. The latest Oracle versions are Oracle Database 19c (Long Term Release), 21c (Innovation Release), and the new 23c (Long Term Release).

Q8.What language does Oracle use?

Ans. Oracle Database developers can use different programming languages to create applications with C++, Java, COBOL, PL/SQL, and Visual Basic.

Q9.Who is the CEO of Oracle?

Ans. Safra Catz is the CEO of Oracle Corporation. She has been a board member of Oracle since 2001.

Q10.Does Oracle have two CEOs?

Ans. In September 2014, Oracle said that Ellison would no longer be the CEO, and instead, Mark Hurd and Catz would become co-CEOs. Then, in September 2019, Catz became the only CEO after Hurd left because of health problems.

Q11.Who is the head of Oracle India?

Ans. Shailender Kumar is the head of Oracle India
