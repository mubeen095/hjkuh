When It Comes To Mobile How Would You Define Usability

Ans. Option B) The visitors experience on your site is the Correct Answer

The most important thing on smartphones and mobile devices is how visitors feel when they use your website or app. If they have a good experience, they’ll stay longer and come back. But if their experience is bad, you might lose them as customers. So, making sure your visitors have a great time on your site is crucial for success.
