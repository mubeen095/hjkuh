These recorded actions are then used as the basis for creating automation scripts or “bots” that can replicate the same actions with high speed and accuracy.

Process recorders are particularly useful for automating repetitive and rule-based tasks. They record various user interactions, such as mouse clicks, keystrokes, data entry, and other actions performed on a computer’s interface. This recorded data is often translated into a script or workflow that RPA bots can execute.
