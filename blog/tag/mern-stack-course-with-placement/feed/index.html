Placement Offered For Mern Stack

During the 5th and 6th months of the program, the focus will be on placement training. In this phase, learners will receive training to develop essential soft skills that enhance their performance during job placements. The training includes:

A) Resume building sessions
B) Mock interviews with fellow candidates
C) Group discussions
D) Soft skills training
E) Minimum of 3 real-time interviews with corporate companies.

This comprehensive training aims to prepare learners for successful job placements by refining both their technical and interpersonal skills.
