Oracle Recruitment Process For Freshers 2024

Oracle Recruitment Process has 3-4 rounds to recruit freshers in the role of Software Engineers. This involves an online Test, Technical Interview Round 1, Technical Interview Round 2, and HR Round.

1. Online test

They evaluate the candidates’ aptitude, problem-solving abilities, and technical knowledge. Candidates who perform well in this round progress to the next stage.

2. Technical Interview

In technical Interviews, candidates face interviews with experienced software professionals from Oracle. During this round, their technical expertise, coding skills, and understanding of programming concepts are thoroughly evaluated.

If successful in the previous round, candidates move on to the Technical Interview Round 2, which often involves more in-depth technical questions, coding challenges, and discussions about their approach to problem-solving.

3. HR Round

In the HR Round, candidates will interact with human resources professionals from Oracle. This round focuses on assessing the candidate’s soft skills, communication abilities, cultural fit, and overall suitability for the company’s work environment.

Throughout this multi-round process, Oracle aims to identify the best talents who possess the necessary technical skills, problem-solving abilities, and personal attributes to excel as Software Engineers within the company.

Recruiting candidates is done by hiring managers, recruiters, support staff, and HR. The recruiting panel will have tools to create and manage job requisitions, and source and nurture candidates. Initially, they will screen candidates to enhance the hiring process. Screening is done while selecting the resumes of candidates. Later, the hiring manager will analyze the candidate’s skills through the selection process. Hiring managers will create and manage job offers. Further, if they get selected in the Oracle recruitment process, they will be given an offer letter and will be informed about the Onboarding process in Oracle.

Onboarding will give you a unified, automated, and customizable system to choose new hires and employees. Onboarding will further allow candidates to complete documentation and give an introduction to the probation period. Finally, recruiters will check the background of the candidates and will hire them.

Oracle Recruitment Process for Experienced 2024

The interview process typically consists of multiple rounds, starting with a screening interview followed by technical and managerial interviews. The step-by-step process of the Oracle Recruitment Process in 2024 is added below.

Round 1 (Screening Interview)

This is usually a call with the HR team to understand your background, experience, and the role you’ve applied for. They will discuss the project details, required skills, and technology stack.

Round 2 (Technical Interview)

In this round, you’ll have a video call with a technical interviewer. They will review your resume and ask about your current project and the technologies you’re familiar with. You’ll also be asked to solve coding problems related to data structures and algorithms and explain your approach.

Round 3 (Technical Interview)


This round may involve a combination of data structure and algorithm questions, as well as coding/conceptual questions related to a specific programming language (e.g., Java). You may be asked to solve problems using recursive approaches and answer questions on topics such as exception handling, multithreading, and collection frameworks.

Round 4 (Managerial Interview)

In this round, you’ll meet with the hiring manager or a senior team member. They will discuss the project’s impact and your experience. You may be asked to explain the high-level architecture of your current project, design patterns, caching mechanisms, microservices, and Spring Boot concepts.

Round 5 (Director Round Interview)

This round typically involves behavioral questions related to conflict resolution, team leadership, strengths, weaknesses, and your motivation for applying. You may also be asked to code a real-world business use case.

Round 6 (HR Interview)

After completing the previous rounds, you’ll have a final discussion with HR regarding your salary expectations, notice period, and other logistics. If selected, you’ll receive an offer letter.

Remember, the interview process can vary depending on the company and role, but this general structure should give you an idea of what to expect. Preparation and practice are key to performing well in these interviews.

Oracle hiring Process Time 2024

The Oracle interview process typically begins with a phone interview to learn about your background and interest in the role. Next, you will complete an online skills assessment and tests to evaluate your technical abilities. If you pass these, you will move on to two technical interviews where your technical skills and problem-solving abilities are further tested. Finally, there is an HR interview to discuss your fit with the company and the role. On average, the entire hiring process at Oracle takes between 33 to 41 days from start to finish. This thorough process ensures that Oracle hires the best candidates for their team.

Oracle Recruitment Proces 2024- FAQs

Q1. What roles are Oracle hiring for in 2024?

Ans. Oracle is recruiting for various roles, including software developers, data scientists, product managers, business analysts, sales professionals, and technical support engineers.

Q2.What is the typical recruitment process at Oracle?

Ans. The recruitment process generally involves several stages:
Online application
Initial screening (resume and cover letter review)
Online assessments (aptitude, technical, and sometimes psychometric tests)
Technical interviews (could be multiple rounds)
HR interview
Offer and onboarding

Q3. How should I prepare for the technical interview?

Ans. Prepare by reviewing key concepts in your field, practicing coding problems (if applicable), and understanding Oracle’s products and services. Familiarize yourself with common interview questions and consider doing mock interviews.

Q4.What is the format of the technical interview?

Ans. The technical interview can include whiteboard coding, system design questions, and problem-solving tasks. For non-technical roles, expect case studies or situational questions related to the role.

Q5.How long does it take to hear back after an interview?

Ans. It typically takes 1-2 weeks to hear back, but this can vary based on the role and the number of candidates interviewed.

Q6. What should I expect in the job offer?

Ans. The job offer will include details about the position, salary, benefits, start date, and other terms and conditions of employment.

Q7.What benefits does Oracle offer its employees?

Ans. Oracle offers a comprehensive benefits package that may include health insurance, retirement plans, stock options, wellness programs, and professional development opportunities.

Q8. What is the onboarding process like?

Ans. Onboarding at Oracle typically involves orientation sessions, training programs, and meetings with team members and managers to help new hires integrate into the company culture and their specific roles.

Q9. How important is cultural fit in Oracle’s hiring process?

Ans. Cultural fit is important as Oracle values teamwork, innovation, and a collaborative work environment. Demonstrating alignment with these values can enhance your candidacy.

Q10.Are there opportunities for career growth at Oracle?

Ans. Yes, Oracle encourages career development through internal mobility, professional development programs, mentorship, and training opportunities.
