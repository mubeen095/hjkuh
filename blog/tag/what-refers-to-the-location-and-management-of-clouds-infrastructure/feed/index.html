What Refers To The Location And Management Of Clouds Infrastructure

Ans. Option B) Deployment is the Correct Answer

The term that refers to the location and management of cloud infrastructure is:

b. Deployment

Cloud deployment models define where and how cloud computing resources are physically located, managed, and made available to users. These models include public cloud, private cloud, hybrid cloud, multi-cloud, community cloud, and edge cloud, each with its own characteristics and management considerations.
