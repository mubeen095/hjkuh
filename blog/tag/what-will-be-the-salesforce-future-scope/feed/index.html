Salesforce’s future scope is bright and evolving. It involves cloud computing growth, AI integration, expansion into various industries, and a focus on providing a unified customer view. The ecosystem will continue to grow, enabling more customization and collaboration. Security, compliance, and low-code development will be emphasized, and global expansion will continue. Salesforce’s commitment to innovation ensures a competitive edge in the CRM and business software market.
