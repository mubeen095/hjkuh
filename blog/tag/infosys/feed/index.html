What is Infosys Exam 2024?

The Infosys exam is a recruitment process conducted by Infosys, a leading global IT services company based in India. This comprehensive examination is a gateway for aspiring candidates to secure employment opportunities within the organization. What is the Infosys Interview Process?

To get a job at Infosys, you need to go through these steps:

  1. Online Test: First, you’ll have to take an online exam. This test will check your skills – logical thinking, English, math, and coding (depending on the job role). The questions can be quite difficult.
  2. Technical Interview: If you clear the online test, you’ll be called for a technical interview. Here, experts from Infosys will assess your technical knowledge and problem-solving abilities related to the job role.
  3. HR Interview: The final round is an interview with Infosys’s human resources team. They will evaluate your overall personality, communication skills, and fitment for the company’s culture.

Performing well in all three rounds is crucial to get hired by Infosys. The online test and interviews are designed – to assess your skills and potential for the job.

Infosys Exam -FAQs

Q1. What is Infosys?

Ans. Infosys is a global leader in consulting, technology, and outsourcing solutions. It provides services, application development, product engineering, infrastructure management, and more. Infosys is known for its innovative approach to technology and business solutions.

Q2.When was Infosys founded?

Ans. Infosys was founded on July 2, 1981, by Narayana Murthy and six other entrepreneurs in Pune, India.

Q3.Where is Infosys headquartered?

Ans. Infosys is headquartered in Bengaluru, Karnataka, India. It has a global presence with offices and development centers in multiple countries.

Q4.What services does Infosys offer?

Ans. Infosys offers a wide range of services including-
IT Consulting, Application development and maintenance, System Integration, Infrastructure management, Digital transformation, Data Analytics, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning solutions, Blockchain technology, Cloud services and Cybersecurity solutions.

Q5.What industries does Infosys offer?

Ans. Infosys has various industries including banking and financial services, Insurance, Retail, Healthcare, Manufacturing, energy and utilities, Travel and Hospitality Automotive, and Life Sciences.

Q6.How big is Infosys?

Q7.What is Infosys known for?
